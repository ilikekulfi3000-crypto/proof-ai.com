02 / ARCHITECTURE

THE
PIPELINE

Four deterministic steps from raw AI output to a verified Truth Certificate.

📥

STEP 01

INGEST

AI output enters the PROOF stream via API, WebSocket, or direct integration. Any modality accepted.

🔬

STEP 02

EXTRACT

Gemini Live parses the output and isolates every verifiable factual claim using structured function calling.

⚖

STEP 03

VERIFY

Each claim is cross-referenced against Google Search grounding — not another LLM, but indexed, hard sources.

🏆

STEP 04

CERTIFY

A PROOF Score (0–100) and grade is issued. Every claim is labeled VERIFIED, UNCERTAIN, or FLAGGED.

03 / SCORING

THE
FORMULA

A transparent, auditable scoring model. No black boxes.

PROOF_SCORE = (Accuracy × 0.60) + (Coherence × 0.25) + (Confidence × 0.15)

ACCURACY · 60%

60%

COHERENCE · 25%

25%

100 minus the contextual drift score. Measures conversational consistency.

CONFIDENCE · 15%

15%
